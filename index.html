Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 5, 2026

Welcome to Lazy Traveler, developed and operated by Lazy Traveler Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app and related services. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR.

1

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you register or log in via Auth0, we collect your email address and profile information (name, profile picture) provided through your authentication provider.
  • Social Media Content: When you share a social media post URL or text through the App, we process that content to extract travel landmark information on your behalf.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Features used, screens visited, and actions taken.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version for diagnostic purposes.
  • Log Data: Request timestamps, error logs, and performance metrics recorded by our servers.

Information Derived from Your Content

  • We extract landmark and place names from your shared content using AI-powered Named Entity Recognition.
  • The URL or text you submit is sent to our AI provider (OpenAI or Anthropic) and may be temporarily retained on our servers for up to 7 days. Only extracted landmark entities — not the original raw text — are stored beyond that period.
  • We enrich extracted landmarks with publicly available geolocation data from OpenStreetMap and Google Places.

We do not store the raw video content of social media posts on our servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise or real-time GPS location — location sensors are never read by the App.
  • Contacts — we do not access your device contacts.
  • SMS or call logs — we do not access messages or call history.
  • Camera or microphone — we do not access these sensors.
  • Financial or payment information — no in-app purchases or subscriptions exist.
  • Third-party crash analytics — we do not use Firebase, Sentry, or similar SDKs.
2

How We Use Your Information

  • App functionality: Creating and managing your account, processing shared content, delivering landmark discovery features.
  • Personalization: Displaying landmarks relevant to your travel history.
  • Service improvement: Analyzing server-side usage patterns to fix bugs and improve performance.
  • Communications: Sending service-related notifications and responding to support inquiries.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity and unauthorized access.
  • Legal compliance: Meeting applicable legal and regulatory obligations.
We do not use your personal data for advertising and we do not sell your data to third parties.
3

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to provide the services you requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving our services, security, and fraud prevention — where not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal Obligation: Where required to comply with applicable law.
4

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with the following trusted service providers:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Auth0 User authentication and identity management Email, profile data
OpenAI / Anthropic AI-powered entity extraction from shared content URL or text of shared posts (temporary, up to 7 days)
OpenStreetMap / Nominatim Geolocation enrichment of landmarks Place name queries only
Google Places API Fallback geolocation enrichment Place name queries only
Cloud Hosting Provider Infrastructure and data storage Encrypted data at rest
We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Sentry, Mixpanel, or any third-party analytics or crash reporting service. We have no payment processor as we offer no paid features.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or valid legal process (e.g., a court order).

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for the lifetime of your account; deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Shared URL / text content: Retained for up to 7 days, then deleted.
  • Share requests: Automatically expire after 7 days.
  • Landmark data: Retained as part of your travel history until you delete it or your account.
  • Server logs: Retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging.
6

Data Security

  • Encryption in transit: All data is encrypted using TLS.
  • Encryption at rest: Personal data in our databases is encrypted at rest.
  • Access controls: Access is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • Authentication: Accounts secured via Auth0 with OAuth 2.0 support.
  • Regular reviews: Periodic security reviews of our infrastructure.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7

Your Rights

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limits on how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lazytravel.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

8

Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data by:

  1. Email: Send a request to privacy@lazytravel.ai with subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. In-App: Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete Account (if available in your version).

We will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at privacy@lazytravel.ai and we will promptly delete it.

10

Third-Party Links and Services

The App may display information about third-party services (e.g., landmarks with links to their websites). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

11

International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission to ensure your data remains protected.

12

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via an in-app notification on your next login and by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

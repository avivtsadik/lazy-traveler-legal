Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Lazy Traveler account and all associated personal data at any time, free of charge.

Last updated: March 5, 2026

This includes your account profile, saved landmarks, travel history, and any personal data associated with your account.

How to request deletion

1

Send an email to privacy@lazytravel.ai with the subject line Account Deletion Request.

2

Include the email address associated with your account so we can identify it.

3

We may ask you to verify account ownership to protect your data from unauthorized deletion requests.

4

We will delete or anonymize your account and all personal data within 30 days.
